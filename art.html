The Arts District of L.A. is a section of downtown that is busting with new restaurants and galleries. It is also home artwork created by some of the most influential artists of our time. Here are just a few of the pieces that you may find around town. If you do, stop and check out the neighrhood, you will definitely find something that you didn't see before.



RISK - A staple of the L.A. art scene, he has also painted the wall at Art Center. You can find this particular wall on the side of the new Arts District Brewery. Grab a pint of your favorite beer and enjoy some great art too.




banksy - The anonymous artist from the UK has left his mark in Los Angeles. His popularity roase with the making of the documentary Exit the Gift Shop. If you find yourself near this Parking Lot, stick around awhile it might just be a park.
