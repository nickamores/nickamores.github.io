7 Simple Steps

  1. Boil Water
  2. Place paper filter in AeroPress
  3. place ground beans in AeroPress to desired lever
  4. Fill AeroPress with boiling water
  5. Insert plunger of AeroPress
  6. Press plunger until all of the water is expelled
  7. Enjoy your fresh cup of hot coffee!
